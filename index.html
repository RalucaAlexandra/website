Projects

Arduino Project - Weather station using a LED cube

The project consists of a cube of 64 LEDs that will play light games controlled by two sensors: temperature and humidity sensor, light intensity sensor. The LED cube consists of 64 LEDs arranged in 4 layers (lines) of 16 LEDs each (columns). The humidity sensor and the light sensor will generate inputs that will be processed by the ATmega2560 microprocessor, which based on them will generate, in the end, the corresponding play of lights of the LED cube.
